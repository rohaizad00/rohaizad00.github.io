Ziarah Mahabbah

Overview

Ziarah Mahabbah is a community outreach program that focuses on visiting asnaf students of Universiti Putra Malaysia (UPM) to strengthen relationships and provide moral support. Through this program, we offer basic necessities and financial assistance to help ease their burden and show care for their well-being and education.

What We Do

  • Conduct home visits to asnaf students of Universiti Putra Malaysia (UPM) across Malaysia.
  • Provide basic necessities and financial assistance to support students’ daily needs.
  • Offer moral support and encouragement to motivate students in their academic journey.
  • Strengthen relationships between the university community and students’ families.

My Role

  • Coordinated and confirmed student home visits.
  • Planned visit routes and schedules to ensure smooth execution.
  • Reviewed accommodation arrangements and ensured all logistical requirements were met.
  • Documented the program by capturing photographs and videos for records and reporting purposes.
  • Served as the driver, ensuring safe and timely transportation throughout the visits.
